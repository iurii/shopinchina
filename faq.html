Delivery Payment Faq About us

What type of payments do you support ?  
I accept payments via Western Union and UnionPay. In case of UnionPay you simply put money on my card - Merchants Bank or China Construction Bank. If you live in China, you might consider using UnionPay, as there will be no fee for the transfer.

Which delivery services do you use ?  
I send products using China Post or EMS (30% discount).

What is the fee for your service ?  
It depends on the amount of stuff you want to buy. The more you buy, the more discount you have. We charge 10% of the product price, if the total sum of the order is less then 1000 RMB.
We charge 8% if your order is more than 1000 RMB.
