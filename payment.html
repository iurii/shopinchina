Delivery Payment Faq About us

What type of payments do you support?
I accept payments via Western Union and UnionPay. In case of UnionPay you simply put money on my card - Merchants Bank or China Construction Bank.

Why don't you use PayPal?

I used to use it, but its work is unstable in China. Often PayPal accounts in China are locked, so I decided to stay with Western Union for a while, though I will do my best to make it work and provide ability to pay with PayPal.

What is the fee for your service?

It depends on the amount of stuff you want to buy. The more you buy, the more discount you have. We charge 10% of the product price, if the total sum of the order is less then 1000 RMB.
We charge 8% if your order is 1000-3000 RMB.
We charge only 5% if your order is more than 5000 RMB.
